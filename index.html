Birthday Calculator

This web application's function is to calculate
the specific day in which a user was born,
then calculate their
Akan name(A Ghanaian naming system where
a person is named according to the day of the week
when they were born.Each day has a different Akan name
and are different by gender.)
The website provides a form which includes the
year, month, and day of birth and gender sections.
Users will key in their details and after they click submit
their Akan name will be displayed.
If wrong data is fed into the form, an invalid message is displayed.
